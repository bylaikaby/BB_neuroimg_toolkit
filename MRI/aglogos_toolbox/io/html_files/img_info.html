  PURPOSE : To read information about 'IMG' formatted data.
  USAGE    : [nx,ny,nslice,nobs,samptime,obslens,dx,dy,thick,datatype] = img_info(imgfile)
  ARGOUTS : 'samptime' in seconds.    'obslens' as in volumes
