MN_TCSLICE_LOAD_SAG - Loads time course of given SAGITAL slice.
    TCIMG = MN_TCSLICE_LOAD_SAG(SESSION,GRPNAME,S_SLICE,[REALIGNED=1]) loads
    time course of given SAGITAL slice as tcImg structure.
    [TCIMG, MATFILE] = MN_TCSLICE_LOAD_SAG(SESSION,GRPNAME,S_SLICE,[REALIGNED=1]) also
    loads and returns tcImg in addition to its associated filename.

    EXAMPLE :
        tcImg = mn_tcslice_load_sag('d03se1','mdeftinj',10);  % load realigned tcImg of slice 10
        tcImg = mn_tcslice_load_sag('d03se1','mdeftinj',36);  % load tcImg of slice 36
        mn_tcslice_load_sag('d03se1','mdeftinj',62);                  % assign tcImg into "caller" workspace.
