[REALIGNMENT/MOTION CORRECTION]
  In general, it doesn't change things dramatically but it difinitely improve.
  Recommended parameter for spm_realign() is
    flags.fwhm : about 2.5 times of voxel resolution
    flags.sep  : aoubt 2.0 times of voxel resolution

[PCA-BASED DENOISE]
  Mean time course of many voxels doesn't change much, but time course of individual 
  voxel become smoother.

[NORMALIZATION]
  Simple division of each time point by that of...
  "global"     :  time course become smooth.
  "contra opn" :  moderate
  "pituitary"  :  not good, time course become noisy.


[PROJECTION OUT]
  It is difficult to say which a region is good for projection out.
  Usually "muscle" or "cerebellum" looks better while other regions, like
  "pituitary" suppresses signals too much.
  In addition to this, "projection out" may not work as we expected because
  there is no guarantee that a "subtracting" component is orthogonal to desired
  and remaining components.

[TTEST]
  Spatial XY filtering removed random "significant" voxels greatly.
